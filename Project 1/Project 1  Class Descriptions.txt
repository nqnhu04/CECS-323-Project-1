Class Descriptions:

1. Film - A Film is displayed in a specific Room inside a Theater for a certain Showing.
2. Theater - A Theater is a large building that has many Rooms that show multiple
3. Showings of multiple Films.
4. Room - A Room is the specific location in a Theater that shows many Showings of
many Films.
5. Seat - A Seat is located within a Room at a Theater that is reserved with a Ticket to
view a specific Showing of a Film.
6. Showing - A Showing is when a Film is shown at a specific Room at a Theater.
7. Ticket - A Ticket selects a Seat from the Room at a Theater for a specific Showing of a
Film
